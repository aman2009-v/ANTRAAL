About ANTRAL MUN

Welcome to ANTRAL MUN - The First Hour, where diplomacy meets determination. As one of the premier Model United Nations conferences, we bring together passionate delegates from across the globe to debate, discuss, and deliberate on pressing international issues.

Our conference challenges delegates to step into the shoes of world leaders, diplomats, and policymakers, fostering critical thinking, public speaking, and negotiation skills. With diverse committees including UNHRC, UNGA, AIPPM, and LOK SABHA, every participant finds their voice in the pursuit of global solutions.
